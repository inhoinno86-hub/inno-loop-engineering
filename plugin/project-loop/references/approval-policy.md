# Approval Policy

Explicit user approval is required for Design, Roadmap, implementation plan, Superpowers planning, separate Superpowers execution, material scope or architecture change, destructive or external actions, milestone closeout, and scope-changing resume from `BLOCKED`.

Record approval in the relevant artifact under `## Decision Log` as `- YYYY-MM-DD: APPROVED - decision - User`.

Superpowers remains task-scoped. Planning approval never enables execution. Routine work inside an approved plan may continue without repeated approval unless repository policy adds a gate. Commit, push, merge, deploy, publish, delete, and branch operations require their own explicit request.
